There's nothing better than a bit of local knowledge when you're planning a summer trip. So we've gathered some top insider tips from locals along our routes. Not the obvious stuff either — these are proper hidden gems for you to discover and show off on Instagram.

The Northern Quarter
Their website toolbar says it all: EATS. DRINKS. ARTS. SHOPPING. You can see why it's a local favourite.
Skyliner Street Art Tour
Arguably the best and definitely the coolest way to learn about the city's modern history. Instagram gold.
The BEST burgers
If you're after some of the juiciest burgers and best meat grills in town – these three should be at the top of your hit list.
Birkenhead Market
With literally everything from boutiques and bars to fishmongers and an outdoor flea market, you wouldn't need to leave this place!
Beatles Bike Tour
An amazing 16-mile guided ride around their old haunts and off-the-beaten-track locations. Probably the best summer day out in history.
Chinese food heaven
Everyone loves a Chinese, but no one more than Liverpudlians. We've been recommended three of the best…
Aira Force Waterfall
Step off the well-trodden walks and see the power of nature at its absolute best. This really is a spectacular sight.
Lake Cruises
AIt may sound an obvious thing to do in 'the Lakes', but we have it on good authority that there's no better way to see them in the summer.
Country pubs
Hot food, local ales, incredible panoramic views – when it comes to good pubs, the locals definitely know best.
